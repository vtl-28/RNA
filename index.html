ABOUT

Refentse Nanny Agency was established to help parents who have trouble finding babysitters to take care of their young ones while they are maybe too busy with other responsibilities. Refentse Nanny Agency specializes in part-time babysitting in order to put parents’ concerns and worries at ease about their kids’ whereabouts while they are temporarily unavailable or have other responsibilities to take care of at that particular time.

Refentse Nanny Agency realized the reality and need that as a parent you cannot be at two places at once and at times it gets difficult to find someone to look after your kids in your temporary absence and Refentse Nanny Agency is all about addressing that problem and making parents’ lives easier. As the founder of Refentse Nanny Agency I am a parent of three myself and I know the struggle of finding someone trustworthy to take of your kids as if they were their own in your absence, but with Refentse Nanny Agency a lot of parents like me can finally stop worrying about the safety of their young ones in their absence.
